HoopUp · Court Favorites

Bookmark any court with a star, then jump back to your saved courts from a new Favorites tab on your profile. Rendered in the live app theme. Two surfaces: the save toggle on the court detail page (with all its states) and the Favorites list with populated / empty / loading states.

Surface 1 · Court detail

Save toggle on a court

A star pill sits beside the court name. Tap to save / un-save (optimistic — flips instantly, reverts on error). Signed-out viewers see a quiet "Sign in to save" link instead.
